What We Do

At Summit Neuro, we blend neuroscience, exercise science, and high-tech analytics to find the why behind your symptoms and performance plateaus—then build a plan to rebalance your brain-body circuits. Our Spryson NeuroAI and NeuroSync technology map how your brain, visual, and motor systems communicate so you can function—and feel—better.

How we work

1) Eye–Brain Mapping (Spryson NeuroAI + NeuroSync)

Your eyes are a direct window into brain function. We use advanced eye-movement and reflex analysis to reveal where circuits are inefficient or out of sync.

2) Root-Cause Testing

Validated assessments across balance, vestibular and cerebellar function, cognition, gait/posture, and autonomic regulation help us move beyond “what hurts” to uncover why you feel the way you do.

3) Personalized Neuro-Rehab & Performance Plan

Targeted drills (oculomotor/vestibular training, coordinated movement and balance work, breath and posture integrations) are prescribed to strengthen weak links and optimize the nervous system. We track progress and adjust as you improve.

Why it works

Symptoms name the what we chase the why. Acting as brain-and-body detectives, we identify specific imbalances and address them with precise, measurable interventions—so your time and effort go exactly where they’ll make the greatest difference.
