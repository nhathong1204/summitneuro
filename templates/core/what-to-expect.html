What To Expect

Turning the dysfunctional into the actionable. We synthesize consensus‑level neurology and vestibular guidelines into one comprehensive, measurable evaluation—so you know what’s happening and how we’ll fix it together.

1) Find It

If we can’t find it, we can’t fix it. We investigate neurologic, vestibular, proprioceptive, and visuomotor circuits to locate the slowed connections and faulty pathways—on purpose.

2) Reverse‑Engineer It

We map imbalances and weaknesses, then design drills to correct, compensate, or train through them—so function improves in the right places.

3) Optimize It

We build therapeutic strategies to maximize gains, re‑test frequently, and maintain momentum with clear measurables.

When progress varies:

Many factors can slow recovery—prior injuries and concussions, infections or autoimmune issues, genetic and metabolic factors, hormone imbalance, dietary/environmental triggers, low cardiovascular training, insulin resistance/low insulin sensitivity, low HRV, blood‑pressure issues, medication side effects, poor sleep, over‑training/over‑thinking, and polypharmacy (≥5 meds/day). Neuroplasticity needs repetition and consistency—we’ll coach you through it.
