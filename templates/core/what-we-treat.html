Summit Neuro

What We Treat

At Summit Neuro, we target the root causes behind your neurological, balance, and performance challenges—not just the symptoms. Using our Spryson NeuroAI and NeuroSync analytics, we identify disrupted brain-body communication patterns, then design personalized programs to restore clarity, coordination, and control.

Concussion & TBI

Our advanced neuro-functional exam and Spryson NeuroAI analytics identify disrupted neural circuits after concussion or head injury.

Using NeuroSync technology, we retrain precise brain-body pathways to restore balance, focus, and performance.

Dizziness & Balance Disorders

We combine vestibular and ocular tracking tests with Spryson NeuroAI pattern analysis to uncover the true source of imbalance.

NeuroSync recalibration then strengthens neural coordination and spatial awareness for lasting stability.

Brain Fog & Cognitive Performance

Through detailed neuro-cognitive evaluation and AI-driven mapping, we detect subtle inefficiencies in brain processing.

NeuroSync optimization sessions restore clarity, energy, and sharper mental performance.

Dysautonomia

Our comprehensive autonomic testing integrates NeuroAI analytics to assess and map heart-rate, vascular, and energy regulation patterns.

NeuroSync neuromodulation restores rhythm, improves regulation, and supports daily resilience.

Eye Movement & Visual-Motor Issues

We perform precision eye-tracking exams interpreted through Spryson NeuroAI to locate visual timing or coordination deficits.

NeuroSync retraining refines ocular control, depth perception, and smooth visual motion.

Multiple Sclerosis & Neurological Disorders

Our integrated NeuroAI-guided assessment identifies specific communication gaps between brain and body.

Targeted NeuroSync rehabilitation enhances mobility, coordination, and endurance to help you move with confidence.
